Where is the U.S. Green Party Represented?

The Green Party may be a political minority, but that doesn't mean Greens don't get elected.

Each green dot is a currently serving Green in an elected office. Toggle the "Voter Information" layer to see what percentage of voters for each state are registered Greens. States outlined in green are states where the Green party has ballot access in presidential elections.
